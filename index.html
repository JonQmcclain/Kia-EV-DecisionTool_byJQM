Kia EV Advisor

Project 4 Measurements & Analytics

Group 3: Emily Kenney, Felix Garcia, Jon Q. Mcclain, Olivia Burns

Interactive EV decision-support prototype showing how Kia can reduce purchase uncertainty through EV fit quizzes, cost calculators, charging planners, and KPI tracking.

Prototype Purpose

This prototype demonstrates how Interactive Decision Tools can move shoppers from awareness to consideration, dealer engagement, test drive, and conversion while generating measurable marketing analytics.

Measurement Logic

Awareness -> landing page visits and campaign clicks

Consideration -> quiz starts, completion rate, calculator usage, charging planner usage

Dealer Action -> saved results, dealer appointment clicks, EV specialist requests

Test Drive -> scheduled test drives and completed test drives

Conversion -> EV purchases or leases attributed to tool engagement
